# Hi, I'm Xigang Sun

Ph.D. student in Computer Science and Technology at Southeast University. My research focuses on **spatio-temporal forecasting**, **social network analysis**, and **graph neural networks**, with a growing interest in **AI for Science** and **LLM/Agent systems**.

> AI for Science · Spatio-temporal Data Mining · Graph Neural Networks · Information Diffusion

## About Me

- Ph.D. student, School of Computer Science and Engineering, Southeast University, Nanjing, China
- M.S. in Computer Science and Technology, Soochow University
- B.S. in Computer Science and Technology, Shandong Normal University
- Email: [xigangsun@seu.edu.cn](mailto:xigangsun@seu.edu.cn)

## Research Interests

- **Spatio-temporal forecasting:** traffic prediction, long-term forecasting, frequency-aware modeling
- **Social network analysis:** information diffusion, cascade prediction, popularity prediction
- **Graph neural networks:** dynamic graph learning, heterogeneous graph modeling, spatial-temporal graph learning
